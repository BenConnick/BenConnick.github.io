"I want to expand what a video game can be."




Journey was, in many ways, a game-changer for the industry (pun intended). For example, efforts to facilitate easy communication in games are thought to increase player interaction, but the decision not to include chat functionality during the game actually brought people much closer. What Journey did was more significant than just break many of the deeply established conventions of the industry. The game was a testament to the possibilities of the medium. Journey won Game of the Year at D.I.C.E, IGN, and Gamespot, as well as dozens of other awards. This fourteen person studio showed the world that there is so much potential in games as a medium.
