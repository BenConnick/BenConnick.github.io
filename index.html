As the cats come pouring in, the player will have to find somewhere to put them, and remember which cat belongs to which owner...

My Role:

Puzzle Force

Puzzle Force was a Match-3 Puzzle RPG mobile game by PerBlue. [video]

Players unlock and level up new heroes, using their powerful abilities to win match 3 battles in campaign and ranked multiplayer. Features full 3D environments and characters, as well as a a 3D base to build and upgrade. Was soft-launched in limited Beta on Google Play and the App Store.

My Role:
I was a UI developer on Puzzle Force from the earliest prototypes through the game's soft launch more than a year later.

Portal Quest

Portal Quest was a mobile RPG by PerBlue.

Players collect heroes and level them up to complete an epic campaign and compete in tournaments, contests, and guild wars. Portal Quest features a quirky style and a quirkier cast. Was available on Google Play and the App Store.

My Role:
I was a UI developer on Portal Quest for a year. Examples of tasks I worked on: 'Contests' and 'Portal Lords' screens, chat "sticker" system (lets players use unique, unlockable emojis in the chat).
